Historic places in my city

Botanical Gardens

  • A peaceful oasis close to the city
  • Home to the largest collection of plant specimens in South Africa
  • more than a million species catalogued and stored here

Union Buildings

  • South Africans after former President Nelson Mandela's inauguration took place here in 1994
  • command the highest point of the city on Meintjieskop Hill near historic Church Square
  • It's the seat of government and also houses the offices of the president and the state archives

Pretoria Zoo

  • Provides a full day of animal fun for the family
  • Among the large mammal collection, highlights include the lion and tiger exhibit, hippos, and elephants
  • aquarium where visitors can see many species of fish including ragged tooth sharks
